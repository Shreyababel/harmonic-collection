Places to find the Best Street Art is New York

First Street Green Art Park

More than a decade after First Park's revival, the community transformed an abandoned section into a vibrant space with gardens, a plaza, and murals, thanks to artist Jonathan Neville's vision.

The LISA Project Street Art

The LISA Project has been curating iconic street art across NYC for over a decade, with murals like Shepard Fairey’s Blondie and Tristan Eaton’s Audrey Hepburn becoming legendary.

The New Allen Street Art

The New Allen, a passion project by Franco Noriega and Milan Kelez, celebrates street art's power to spark conversations and unite communities.

Freeman Alley Street Art

Freemans Alley, once a raw haven for street artists, now blends its gritty past with creative modernity, symbolizing the Bowery's gentrification.

The Museum of Street Art

The Museum of Street Art at CitizenM Bowery Hotel preserves the vibrant legacy of 5Pointz, showcasing works from its iconic artists across 20 floors of immersive graffiti art.

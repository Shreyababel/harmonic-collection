Vintage and Thrift stores

Awoke Vintage

A hidden gem in Williamsburg, Awoke Vintage specializes in carefully curated, timeless pieces from the ’80s and ’90s. Known for its eclectic mix and friendly, knowledgeable staff, it’s a local favorite for one-of-a-kind finds in clothing and accessories.

9th Street Vintage

Nestled in the East Village, 9th Street Vintage has a warm, nostalgic vibe and is a haven for those seeking pre-1980s clothing. This shop is a favorite among locals for its handpicked items that tell stories of past decades. From classic denim to unique jackets, 9th Street Vintage specializes in quality, true vintage pieces. With friendly staff who know each piece well, this store feels like a cozy, curated time capsule.

Amarcord Vintage Fashion

Founded by Italian natives, Amarcord Vintage Fashion brings European elegance to NYC’s vintage scene. Known for its high-end selection of vintage designer clothing and accessories from the ’50s through the ’90s, it’s a go-to for fashion-savvy locals. Both the SoHo and Williamsburg locations offer a chic yet approachable shopping experience, with a mix of avant-garde pieces and rare designer finds, from Chanel to Yves Saint Laurent. It’s a perfect spot for those looking to add some luxury vintage to their wardrobe.

Dear:Rivington

Dear: Rivington+ is a hidden gem for those who love artful, avant-garde fashion. Located on Rivington Street, this shop offers a refined, gallery-like atmosphere and specializes in unique, one-of-a-kind pieces that locals adore for their craftsmanship and design. With a focus on high-end vintage and art-inspired fashion, Dear: Rivington+ carries designer pieces from the likes of Comme des Garçons and Yohji Yamamoto. It’s a destination for those who want their wardrobe to reflect personal style, artistic expression, and rarity.

WGACA

This iconic vintage shop in SoHo is famous for its extensive collection of luxury vintage items, especially its highly coveted designer handbags and accessories. Locals flock here for timeless pieces from brands like Chanel, Louis Vuitton, and Hermès, all carefully authenticated. The store has a sophisticated vibe, with everything from vintage rock tees to couture finds. What Goes Around Comes Around is a favorite for those seeking quality, luxury, and a taste of history wrapped up in classic designer labels.
